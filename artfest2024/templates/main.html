Registration Help
Step 1:

You can see the Signup/Register Button on the top right corner. Click on it to register for Arts Fest.

Step 2:

If you are new, click "Register" to provide your details. Already registered? Use "Login" with your admission number and password to access your profile and edit participation categories.

Step 3:

On the "Select Categories" page, choose up to 4 individual and 2 group categories (on stage + off stage).

Step 4:

Verify your registration by checking your name on the Participates List page. You'll also receive a confirmation email for your registered categories. NOTE: Keep the email confirmation with you during the event.
